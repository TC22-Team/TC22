RRPR: Workshop on Reproducible Research in Pattern Recognition

This intended as both a short participative course on the Reproducible Research (RR) aspects, leading to open discussions with the participants, and also as a practical workshop on how to actually perform RR. In addition, another key goal for gathering the research community is to further advance the scientific aspects of reproducibility in pattern recognition research. This workshop is of interest for all ICPR participants and attendees since it allows to handle various topics not restricted to one specific fields. The reproducibility is an important topics in general and particularly good for PhD students and young researchers to learn "good habits".

Main chairs: Daniel Lopresti, Miguel Colom, Bertrand Kerautret, Federico Bolelli
